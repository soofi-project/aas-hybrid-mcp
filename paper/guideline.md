# Paper Writing Guidelines

## Core Contribution
The paper must clearly state the main contribution in the introduction.

The contribution should be:
- novel
- technically meaningful
- clearly distinguishable from prior work

## Problem Definition
The problem must be described before presenting the method.
Explain:
- why the problem matters
- where existing approaches fail.

## Related Work
Related work must:
- cite the most relevant prior methods
- explain differences clearly
- position our approach in the research landscape.

## Method Section
The method must:
- be reproducible
- include algorithm descriptions
- explain design decisions.

## Experiments
Experiments must include:
- strong baselines
- ablation studies
- clear metrics

## Writing Style
- academic tone
- precise language
- avoid marketing language
- avoid vague claims
